# Gasoline
- 87.6 kWh / 10 L
    - https://hypertextbook.com/facts/2003/ArthurGolnik.shtml
- motorcyles ballpark 35 - 40 miles per gallon
    - https://auto.howstuffworks.com/fuel-efficiency/fuel-economy/motorcycle-gas-mileage.htm
    - about half what my truck uses
- I fill up my truck with about 45 L per month (work-days usually)
    - motorcycle would then be about 22 L per month
    - 10 L would give about 10 work-days
    - so about 1 L per day of commuting to work
    - so about 8.76 kWh per day

# Air
- https://www.plantservices.com/articles/2013/04-perfect-compressed-air-system/
    - about 17 kW consumed to get 100 cubic feet per minute of air at 100 PSI
    - 60 cubic feet per minute (of 100 PSI air) consumed to get 1 kW at motor shaft
- 10 L = 0.353147 cubic feet
- 60 cu-ft / min = 1 kW
- 60 cu-ft = 1 kW-minute
- 1699.01 L = 1 kW-minute = 0.016666667 kWh
- 10 L = 0.000098096 kWh
- is...approximately 89299 times smaller than my daily commute!
